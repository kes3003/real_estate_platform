# Capability: Direct Wall Printing

## Overview
Sun Studio provides direct wall printing for large-format graphics, branding, and decorative applications on vertical surfaces.

This service prints directly onto walls without wallpapers, vinyls, or decals, creating a seamless and integrated finish.

## Where This Capability Is Used
- Interior and exterior walls
- Offices and corporate spaces
- Retail stores and showrooms
- Hotels, caf√©s, and hospitality venues
- Murals, branding graphics, and decorative art
- Directional and environmental signage

## Supported Wall Surfaces (Subject to Testing)
- Concrete
- Gypsum and plaster
- Brick
- Wood
- Metal
- Glass
- Painted walls
- Acoustic panels
- Stone
- Plastic and acrylic surfaces

## Key Differentiators
- No size limitation in width
- Prints wall-to-wall with continuous imagery
- Contactless printing with precise alignment
- Compensates for wall unevenness up to 10 cm
- High-resolution, full-color output

## Print Capabilities
- Maximum resolution up to 1200 dpi
- CMYK color spectrum supported
- Optional white ink printing
- UV LED curing for immediate handling

## Size & Scale
- Unlimited print width
- Maximum print height in one pass: 3.5 meters
- Expandable system allows printing up to 4 meters with extensions

## Durability & Performance
- Scratch and abrasion resistant once cured
- Waterproof and solvent resistant
- Indoor prints rated for long-term color stability (reference: up to 12 years indoors)

## Installation & Operation
- Fast setup and on-site printing
- No drilling, fixing, or mounting required
- Minimal disruption to the space during installation

## Important Conditions
- Each surface must be tested before printing
- Outdoor durability depends on environment and exposure
- Some substrates may require pretreatment

## What This Capability Does Not Promise
- No guaranteed lifespan for exterior applications
- No universal compatibility across all wall types
- No structural or architectural guarantees

## Internal Reference
This capability is enabled using the WallPen E2 vertical wall printing system.
