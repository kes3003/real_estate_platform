# Capability: Flatbed Digital & Textured Printing

## Overview
Sun Studio offers flatbed digital printing with advanced textured and raised effects for high-impact visual and tactile applications.

This service is designed for brands and businesses that require precision printing, surface depth, and long-term durability on flat and rigid substrates.

## Key Strengths of This Capability
- High-precision flatbed UV printing
- Raised, textured, and tactile print effects
- Suitable for Braille and tactile signage
- Durable finishes for retail and commercial environments

## Common Applications
- Braille and tactile signage
- Premium advertising and display panels
- Retail branding elements
- Beauty and fragrance brand displays
- Architectural and interior panels

## Supported Substrates (Subject to Testing)
- Acrylic
- Glass
- Metal
- Wood
- Plastic
- Rigid boards and panels
- Mixed-material substrates

## Textured & Raised Printing
- Multi-layer UV printing enables raised and tactile surfaces
- Suitable for Braille applications when correctly configured
- Enables dimensional depth and premium surface effects

## Durability Characteristics
- Alcohol-resistant and scratch-resistant finishes achievable
- Performance depends on surface preparation and pretreatment
- Designed for high-contact retail and commercial use

## Dimensional & Capacity Limits (Reference)
- Maximum printable area: 96 x 48 inches (250 x 130 cm)
- Maximum substrate size: 102 x 58 inches (260 x 150 cm)
- Maximum substrate weight: 10.2 lbs (4.6 kg) per square foot
- Z-axis print height: 12–15 inches (30.48–38.1 cm)

## Accuracy & Control
- X/Y positioning accuracy: ±0.025 mm (±0.001 inch)
- Supports uni-directional and bi-directional printing

## Important Conditions
- All substrates must be tested for adhesion and durability
- Some materials require adhesion promoters or chemical wipes
- Print time and output depend on print mode and configuration

## What This Capability Does Not Promise
- No automatic compliance claims without validation
- No guaranteed durability without testing
- No universal material compatibility

## Internal Reference
This capability is supported by Direct Color Systems flatbed UV printing platforms.
