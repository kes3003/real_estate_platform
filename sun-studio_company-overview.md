# Sun Studio — Company Overview

## Who We Are
Sun Studio is part of a global printing network founded in 2008 by Sun Innovations Company, Russia’s largest manufacturer of UV printers and inks.

The network has grown to more than 130 studios across 21+ countries, combining manufacturing expertise with real-world, production-scale printing experience.

## Sun Studio Dubai
Sun Studio Dubai has been operating since 2011 and serves clients across the MENA region.

The studio delivers high-quality UV, 3D, flatbed, cylindrical, textured, and wall printing services for commercial, architectural, and creative applications.

## What Sun Studio Specializes In
Sun Studio specializes in printing on virtually any surface and material, including:
- Glass
- Metal
- Wood
- Ceramics
- Leather
- Fabric
- Acrylic
- PVC
- Canvas
- Architectural and construction surfaces

## Core Printing Capabilities
Sun Studio provides advanced printing solutions including:
- Cylindrical printing on transparent glass and contoured objects such as perfume bottles, candle jars, and tumblers
- Textured and tactile printing, including Braille and raised signage
- Large-format flat printing for decorative and commercial applications
- Direct wall printing for interiors and commercial environments

## Adhesion & Durability Expertise
Sun Studio is known for its professional adhesion expertise.

Through material testing and specialized pre-treatment solutions, prints can be made:
- Alcohol-resistant
- Scratch-resistant
- Washable
- Durable for real-world commercial use

Final performance depends on material testing and application requirements.
